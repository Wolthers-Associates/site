## 4. Purpose of Video Surveillance / Finalidad de la Videovigilancia

The video surveillance system is used exclusively for the following purposes:

| Purpose | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Security** | Protection of employees, visitors, and company assets |
| **Access Control** | Monitoring entry and exit of personnel and visitors |
| **Incident Prevention** | Deterrence and prevention of theft, vandalism, or unauthorized access |
| **Incident Investigation** | Evidence collection for security incidents when required |
| **Quality Control Lab Security** | Protection of coffee samples and laboratory equipment |

**Prohibited Uses / Usos Prohibidos:**
- Monitoring employee productivity or work performance
- Surveillance of private areas (restrooms, changing rooms)
- Audio recording (unless specifically authorized and disclosed)
- Sharing footage for purposes other than security
- Commercial use of recordings

## 7. Data Retention / Retención de Datos

| Data Type | Retention Period | Justification |
|-----------|------------------|---------------|
| Standard recordings | **30 days** | Security monitoring |
| Incident-related recordings | **Until investigation complete + 1 year** | Legal/investigation needs |
| Recordings requested by authorities | **As required by law** | Legal compliance |

### Deletion Procedure / Procedimiento de Eliminación
1. Recordings are automatically overwritten after 30 days
2. Manual deletion requires Level 1 authorization
3. Deletion must be logged in the Access Log
4. No backup copies retained beyond retention period unless for legal purposes

## 10. Data Subject Rights / Derechos del Titular

Individuals captured by the surveillance system have the following rights under Ley 1581 de 2012:

### Right to Access / Derecho de Acceso
- Individuals may request to view recordings in which they appear
- Request must be submitted in writing to privacy@wolthers.com
- Response within 10 business days
- Viewing provided on-site (no copies unless legally required)

### Right to Information / Derecho a la Información
- Signage informs individuals of surveillance
- Privacy policy available at www.wolthers.com/privacy
- Purpose and controller information displayed at entry points

### Request Handling Procedure / Procedimiento de Solicitudes

1. **Receive Request**
   - Accept written requests at privacy@wolthers.com
   - Verify identity of requester

2. **Evaluate Request**
   - Confirm requester appears in recordings
   - Check if recordings still exist (within retention period)
   - Assess if third-party rights affected

3. **Respond**
   - Within 10 business days
   - Provide viewing appointment OR
   - Explain why request cannot be fulfilled

4. **Document**
   - Log all requests and responses
   - Maintain records for 2 years

## 11. Incident Response / Respuesta a Incidentes

### Security Incident Involving Video Evidence

1. **Identification**
   - Document date, time, location of incident
   - Identify relevant camera(s)

2. **Preservation**
   - Immediately preserve relevant recordings
   - Mark as "Legal Hold - Do Not Delete"
   - Document chain of custody

3. **Notification**
   - Notify Office Manager within 24 hours
   - Notify Legal Counsel if law enforcement may be involved
   - Notify HR if employee-related

4. **Investigation**
   - Access only by authorized personnel
   - Document all viewings in Access Log
   - Maintain confidentiality

5. **Resolution**
   - Document outcome
   - Release "Legal Hold" when appropriate
   - Update security measures if needed

### Data Breach Procedure

If unauthorized access to recordings occurs:

1. Immediately secure the system
2. Notify Office Manager and Legal Counsel
3. Document the breach
4. Assess impact on data subjects
5. Notify SIC if required (within 15 business days)
6. Notify affected individuals if high risk
7. Implement corrective measures

## 12. Third-Party Access / Acceso de Terceros

### Law Enforcement Requests

Video recordings may be shared with law enforcement ONLY when:
- A valid court order or legal warrant is presented
- Required by law in an emergency situation
- Approved by Legal Counsel

**Procedure:**
1. Receive written request from authority
2. Verify authenticity of request
3. Consult with Legal Counsel
4. If approved, provide only the specific recordings requested
5. Document in Access Log with case reference number

### Maintenance Providers

Third-party technicians accessing the surveillance system must:
- Sign confidentiality agreement
- Be supervised during access
- Not have unsupervised access to recordings
- Access logged in Access Log
